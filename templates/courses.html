Courses we Offer

This are the lists of courses/subjects offered by the school. For more information on the courses/subjects contact the school via the contact page.

The science courses includes Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Geography, Agricultural Science, Further Mathematics, Technical Drawing, English Language, Mathematics, Economic (Optional for science students), Civic Education, Catering Craft, Yoruba/Ibo Languaage, Computer Studies.
The art courses includes Lit-In-English, C.R.S/I.R.S, Mathematics, English Language, Biology, Government, Food and Nutrition, Civic Education, Yoruba/Igbo Language, Computer Studies.
Mathematics, English Language, Commerce, Accounting, Economics, Biology, Geography, Government, Yoruba/Ibo Language, Food and Nutrition, C.R.S, Civic Education, Computer Studies.

For Junior Secondary School

Mathematics, English Language, A Nigerian Language (Yoruba), Basic Science, Social Studies, Fine Arts/Creative Art, Agricultural Science, Civic Education, Christian Religion Studies, Physical and Health Education, Business Studies, French, Computer Studies, Home Economics, Music, Basic Technology

For Primary School

The subjects approved by the Ministry of Education and taught at primary school level include the following: English Language, Mathematics, Nigerian Languages, Basic Science and Technology (BST), Religion and National Values (RNV), Cultural & Creative Arts (CCA), Arabic Language, Pre-Vocational Studies.

Note that some subjects are compulsory subjects for the science, art and commercial courses. These subjects includes English Language, Mathematics, Civic Education and more. Some are also general optional subjects like Economics, Computer Studies, and more. For more information on the subjects, please contact the school.
